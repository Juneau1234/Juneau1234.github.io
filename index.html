Welcome to Japan!

Japan is a country of fascinating contrasts – a perfect blend of modern technology and rich tradition. From the neon-lit streets of Tokyo to the ancient temples of Kyoto, Japan offers a unique and unforgettable travel experience. Whether you're an adventure-seeker, a culture enthusiast, or a foodie, Japan has something for everyone.

Top Destinations in Japan

Tokyo

The bustling capital city with endless shopping, entertainment, and culture.

Kyoto

A city of beautiful temples, shrines, and traditional tea houses.

Mt. Fuji

The iconic mountain, perfect for hiking or simply enjoying the breathtaking views.

Experience Japanese Culture

Traditional Tea Ceremony

A centuries-old ritual, offering a peaceful way to enjoy green tea.

Calligraphy (Shodo)

Learn the art of Japanese calligraphy, a meditative practice of brushwork.

Sumo Wrestling

Witness the sport of sumo, Japan’s traditional martial art.

Travel Tips for Japan

Transportation

Use the JR Rail Pass for affordable travel across the country by bullet train.

Language

While Japanese is the main language, learning a few phrases will go a long way.

Etiquette

Respect for others is important; always be polite and remove your shoes when entering homes.
